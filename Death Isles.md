A small strip of 7 Islands south west of the center of the map.

**Island 1**: A small island with a few people who were corrupted with spine bone serpents. Shroomy Mgee was turned into a Frankenstein here.

**Island 2**: An island with lots of giant porcupines

**Island 3**:  Volcano island with ash in the air

**Island 4**: A barren Island that the creepy birds have eaten everything

**Island 5**: A living island with many dead corpses in the water

**Island 6**: A large island inhabited with sheet looking things with spheres of destruction on their hands. (on the beach) The Necromantic Cult also had a base on this island.

**Island 7**: A small island near **Island 6** that had 2 black dragons (dead lol) with magic stripping properties.
